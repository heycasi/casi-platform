After 90 Days: Your Options

You now have leverage - choose your path

Option 1: Keep Bootstrapping

Best if: You're profitable, growing steadily, and don't want to give up equity

  • Keep 100% ownership
  • Scale at your own pace
  • Target $10K-20K MRR in next 6 months
  • Profitable lifestyle business
  • No pressure from investors

Option 2: Raise Seed Round

Best if: You want to scale faster and compete with VC-backed competitors

  • Raise $500K-1M at $3-5M valuation
  • Keep 80-90% of company
  • Hire 2-3 key people
  • Accelerate growth to $50K+ MRR
  • Build defensible moat quickly

üí° Pro Tip: You Don't Need to Decide Yet

With $5K MRR and 40%+ growth, you have OPTIONS. Take investor meetings to see what terms you get, but don't feel pressured to take money. The best founders negotiate from a position of strength: "We don't NEED your money, but here's why we might WANT it."
